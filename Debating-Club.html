Debating Club at Bishop Angelo Tarantino Memorial Secondary School

Welcome to the Debating Club

Sharpening Minds. Building Confidence. Inspiring Leaders.

At Bishop Angelo Tarantino Memorial Secondary School, we believe in nurturing the power of critical thinking and eloquent expression. The Debating Club provides a vibrant platform for learners to explore current issues, construct logical arguments, and express their views confidently. Through structured debates and public speaking events, our learners become thoughtful, articulate, and socially aware individuals ready to lead in any field.

What We Offer in Our Debating Club

  • Regular Debate Sessions: Students meet weekly to engage in structured debates on a wide range of topics including education, environment, technology, politics, and social issues.
  • Research & Argument Development: Members are trained to research thoroughly, evaluate multiple perspectives, and present their arguments convincingly.
  • Public Speaking & Confidence Building: Our club emphasizes public speaking drills, confidence exercises, and language mastery, helping students overcome stage fear.
  • Mentorship & Guidance: Teachers and invited guest speakers offer personalized mentorship to help students grow in their oratory and analytical skills.
  • Mock Debates & Competitions: We host intra-school and inter-school debate competitions to expose learners to competitive debating formats and enhance teamwork and leadership.

Benefits of Joining the Debating Club

  • Improved Communication: Learners develop clarity of speech and persuasive language skills essential for academic and life success.
  • Critical Thinking: Debating fosters logical reasoning, problem-solving, and the ability to understand multiple sides of an issue.
  • Self-Confidence: Regular public speaking and performance improve student confidence and interpersonal skills.
  • Leadership Development: Learners take initiative, lead discussions, and represent the school at public forums and competitions.
  • Academic Enrichment: Debate supports learning across subjects, especially in language, social studies, and civic education.
