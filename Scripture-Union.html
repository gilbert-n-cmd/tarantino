Living by the Word of God

The Scripture Union at our school aims to develop students who are grounded in Biblical values, strengthened in faith, and inspired to be servant leaders. Through regular fellowship, Bible study, and prayer, learners are encouraged to live Christ-centered lives and be shining examples both in and outside the school.

Our Purpose

The Scripture Union exists to:

  • Encourage students to read and understand the Bible daily.
  • Help learners build a personal relationship with Jesus Christ.
  • Promote Christian values such as honesty, respect, love, and forgiveness.
  • Equip students to make wise, godly decisions in their daily lives.

Activities We Conduct

We believe faith should be active and engaging. Our SU club organizes:

  • Weekly Fellowship Meetings: Praise and worship, Bible teachings, discussions, and testimonies.
  • Bible Study Sessions: Interactive group studies focused on real-life applications of Scripture.
  • Prayer Meetings: Special times set aside to seek God's presence and intercede for our school and nation.
  • Christian Films & Debates: Fun and thought-provoking events to strengthen Biblical understanding.
  • Outreach Activities: Visits to local communities to demonstrate love and share the gospel through acts of kindness.
  • Annual Scripture Union Weekend: A retreat filled with worship, games, sermons, and spiritual growth.
