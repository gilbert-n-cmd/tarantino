Early Life & Vocation

Bishop Angelo Tarantino was born in 1908 in Italy. He joined the Congregation of the Sacred Heart of Jesus (F.S.C.J.) and later went into missionary work, eventually being sent to Uganda. His mission included cultivating faith, supporting local church structures, and evangelizing in the West Nile region.
