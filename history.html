Bishop Angelo Tarantino Memorial Secondary School was founded with a mission rooted in transformation, empowerment, and academic excellence. Inspired by the legacy of Bishop Angelo Tarantino, the school was established to serve as a beacon of hope and opportunity for learners in Uganda. The Early Years

What began as a modest institution with a few classrooms and a passionate team of educators quickly grew into a thriving center for academic excellence. The school opened its doors with the belief that every child deserves quality education regardless of their background. From its first graduating class, Bishop Angelo Tarantino Memorial Secondary School set high standards in discipline, academic performance, and leadership development.

Growth and Milestones

  • Year 1: School opens with both O-Level and a limited A-Level curriculum.
  • Within 5 Years: Expansion of classrooms, science labs, and dormitory facilities.
  • Year 10: Introduction of advanced A-Level subject combinations and a rise in national exam performance.
  • Community Outreach: Partnered with local leaders to run mentorship programs and scholarships for disadvantaged students.
  • Present Day: Recognized as one of the most respected academic institutions in the region, with a focus on innovation, ethics, and holistic education.
