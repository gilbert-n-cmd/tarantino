Muslim Students' Association

At Bishop Angelo Tarantino Memorial Secondary School, our Muslim community thrives on the values of faith, unity, and academic excellence. The Muslim Students' Association (MSA) is a supportive and inclusive fellowship for all Muslim learners to grow spiritually and morally while excelling in their education.

Unity, Faith, and Knowledge

The MSA aims to nurture the faith of Muslim students by offering a space where they can freely practice their religion, learn about Islamic teachings, and uphold strong moral character. We promote mutual respect, discipline, and brotherhood in line with Islamic principles.

Activities and Programs

Students are encouraged and supported to perform their daily prayers on time. The school provides a clean and peaceful space for prayer and reflection during breaks and lunch hours.

Our Values

  • Faith: Strengthening personal connection with Allah (SWT) through worship and knowledge.
  • Respect: Promoting tolerance, modesty, and good behavior towards others.
  • Excellence: Striving for success in both religious and academic life.
  • Community: Building unity and support among Muslim students in a diverse school environment.

Leadership and Guidance

The MSA is guided by a Muslim teacher patron who mentors students and ensures that all programs align with Islamic principles and school policies. Student leaders are elected annually to organize activities and represent the voice of the Muslim community.

Join Us

Every Muslim learner is welcome to join and actively participate in the MSA. Together, we grow stronger in faith, help one another, and become proud ambassadors of Islam in our school and beyond.
