Board of Governors

Background

The Board of Governors at Bishop Angelo Tarantino Memorial Secondary School plays a critical role in overseeing the strategic direction and governance of the institution. Comprising experienced professionals, educationists, community leaders, and dedicated stakeholders, the Board ensures that the school upholds its mission of providing quality education and maintaining high standards in all areas of academic and operational performance.

Leadership & Responsibility

Chairperson: Mr. John K. Mayombo
Secretary to the Board: The Head Teacher (ex-officio)
Other Members: Representatives from the PTA, Foundation Body, Staff, and Local Government

Governance Structure

The Board operates through various committees focused on finance, academics, welfare, infrastructure, and discipline. Each committee is tasked with guiding school policy, ensuring accountability, and supporting the implementation of government education guidelines and school development goals.

Educational Oversight

The Board ensures the school curriculum is effectively delivered, resources are properly allocated, and learning environments are continuously improved. They evaluate the performance of both teachers and learners, support teacher development programs, and promote innovations that enhance learning outcomes.

Strategic Development

As custodians of the school's long-term vision, the Board members work closely with the administration to plan and implement infrastructure projects, resource acquisition, staff recruitment, and partnerships. They are key to ensuring sustainability and academic excellence at Bishop Angelo Tarantino Memorial Secondary School.

Community Involvement

The Board represents the interests of the community and maintains a strong link between the school and parents, ensuring transparency, accountability, and mutual collaboration. They also promote co-curricular development, moral education, and inclusive practices within the school environment.

The Board of Governors is committed to shaping the future of Bishop Angelo Tarantino Memorial Secondary School through ethical leadership, educational stewardship, and unwavering dedication to student success.
