News & Updates

Shaping the Future Through Progress and Participation

🏫 School Infrastructure Boost – Better Learning Environment

We are excited to announce the completion of a new classroom block, providing additional space to enhance our teaching and learning experience. This development is part of our ongoing mission to create a comfortable and well-equipped environment for both students and teachers.

📚 Academic Achievements – Raising the Bar

Congratulations to our candidates who excelled in their recent exams! Bishop Angelo Tarantino Memorial Secondary School continues to shine in district academic rankings. Our commitment to quality education is reflected in consistent improvement in performance, with special recognition given to top-performing students and hardworking teachers.

🎉 Co-Curricular Excellence – MDD & Sports Highlights

  • MDD Success: Our Music, Dance & Drama (MDD) team impressed at the recent zonal competition, advancing to the district level with a powerful performance celebrating Ugandan heritage.
  • Sports Participation: The school football and netball teams are actively competing in regional tournaments, fostering teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship among our learners.

🌿 Health & Wellness Campaign – Clean School, Healthy Learners

As part of our health initiative, the school hosted a sanitation and hygiene awareness drive. Students participated in cleaning exercises, hand-washing demos, and health talks led by local medical staff. This program ensures that we create a safe and hygienic learning space for everyone.

💡 ICT Integration – Embracing Digital Learning

We are gradually integrating digital tools into our classrooms to promote interactive learning. With support from the Ministry of Education and development partners, select classes have begun using projectors and digital resources to enhance subject delivery and engagement.

🗓️ Upcoming Events

  • Parents' Meeting: Set for June 5th, 2025 – All guardians are encouraged to attend to discuss student progress and school development plans.
  • Cultural Day: Coming in July – A vibrant celebration of Uganda’s cultural diversity through song, dance, and food. All are welcome!
  • Mid-Year Exams: Begin June 20th – Students are encouraged to prepare in earnest. Revision timetables will be shared in classes.

📸 Stay Connected

Follow our social media pages and school notice boards for weekly updates, photos, and highlights of all the exciting things happening at Bishop Angelo Tarantino Memorial Secondary School.

Conclusion

At Bishop Angelo Tarantino Memorial Secondary School, progress is constant. Whether it’s academic milestones, infrastructure improvements, or co-curricular achievements, we remain dedicated to providing holistic, student-centered education. We thank our students, parents, staff, and community for walking this journey with us. Together, we are building a brighter future!
