Co-Curricular Activities

We believe in nurturing every child's potential. Throughout the year, learners participate in:

  • Debate & Public Speaking Competitions
  • Sports and Athletics (Inter-school)
  • Drama, Music and Dance Showcases
  • Educational Trips & Career Days
  • Health Awareness Campaigns

Community Engagement

The school also hosts community events that encourage parent involvement and promote a culture of unity and shared responsibility. These include:

  • School Clean-Up Days
  • Teacher-Parent Meetings
  • Religious Fellowship and Moral Talks
  • Annual Giving & Charity Drives

Stay Informed

Parents and guardians are encouraged to regularly check the notice boards, school WhatsApp groups, and official newsletters for updates and reminders. A downloadable PDF calendar is also available on our website.
