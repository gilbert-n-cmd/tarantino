Catholic Faith & Values at Bishop Angelo Tarantino Memorial Secondary School

Our Catholic Community

Bishop Angelo Tarantino Memorial Secondary School is deeply rooted in Catholic traditions and values. As a faith-based institution, we are committed to nurturing the spiritual, moral, and academic growth of all learners through a Christ-centered education. Our school culture is grounded in the teachings of Jesus Christ, the example of the saints, and the mission of the Church in Uganda.

Daily Spiritual Life

Our day begins and ends with prayer. Learners are encouraged to develop a personal relationship with God through regular Mass attendance, morning devotions, Bible study, and spiritual songs. Catholic traditions are woven into every aspect of school life to foster a sense of faith, reverence, and moral responsibility.

Catechism & Religious Education

Religious education is part of our core curriculum. Catechism classes are conducted weekly to prepare learners for key sacraments such as Baptism, First Holy Communion, and Confirmation. Our aim is to help learners understand Catholic doctrine and apply it in their daily lives.

Pastoral Care & Guidance

Our school chaplain and teachers provide pastoral care and spiritual counseling to learners. We address not just academic performance but also emotional and spiritual well-being. Whenever needed, learners can speak with trusted adults about their concerns, seek prayer support, or receive biblical encouragement.

Catholic Events & Celebrations

The school community actively participates in important Church events such as:

  • Ash Wednesday & Lent Reflections
  • Holy Mass on Feast Days (e.g., St. Mary, St. Joseph, All Saints)
  • The Rosary Month (October) & Marian Processions
  • Christmas and Easter School Celebrations
  • Uganda Martyrs Day Commemorations (June 3rd)

Service and Outreach

Guided by the Catholic Social Teachings, our learners participate in community service activities such as visiting the sick, environmental clean-ups, and charity drives. We teach them to live out values of compassion, justice, humility, and solidarity.

Inclusive and Welcoming

While we are proudly Catholic, our school warmly welcomes learners from all religious backgrounds. Respect for diverse faiths is instilled in every learner, creating a united and peaceful school environment rooted in shared values of love and service.
