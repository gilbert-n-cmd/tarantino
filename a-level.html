A-Level Excellence at Bishop Angelo Tarantino Memorial Secondary School

Shaping Leaders of Tomorrow

The A-Level program at Bishop Angelo Tarantino Memorial Secondary School is designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare learners for university and life beyond the classroom. Our experienced teachers and supportive environment ensure every student maximizes their academic potential while growing into responsible, confident leaders. We provide personalized academic guidance and mentorship so learners can choose subject combinations that align with their career paths.

What is A-Level Education?

Advanced Level (A-Level) is the final two years of secondary education in Uganda, designed for students who have successfully completed O-Level. It focuses on subject specialization and academic depth, preparing students for university admission, professional training, or entrepreneurial ventures. At Bishop Angelo Tarantino Memorial Secondary School, students choose from arts and sciences to suit their interests and future ambitions.

Available Subject Combinations

Our school offers a wide range of subject combinations tailored for both Arts and Science streams. Students are guided in selecting the right subjects based on their abilities and career goals.

  • Science Combinations: PCM, PCB, BCM, MEG, HEG, and more.
  • Arts Combinations: HEG, HLD, HEL, HGK, HED, and more.
  • Subsidiary Subjects: General Paper, ICT, and Sub-Maths (for non-math students).

Why Choose Our A-Level Program?

  • Experienced Teaching Staff: Our teachers are passionate and experienced in delivering subject content in an engaging and exam-oriented manner.
  • Career Guidance: We offer regular career talks, university prep, and guidance to help students make informed choices for their futures.
  • Academic Rigor & Discipline: A focused, disciplined learning environment ensures students stay on track and exceed expectations.
  • Laboratory & Research Work: Practical subjects are supported by well-equipped labs for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Computer Studies.
  • ICT Integration: Our curriculum integrates ICT to enhance digital skills and improve research and learning efficiency.
  • Mock Exams & UNEB Prep: Intensive revision, consultations, and mock exams ensure students are ready for UNEB A-Level exams.
